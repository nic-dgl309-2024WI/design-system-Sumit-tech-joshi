  1. Introduce yourself by sending an email to info@outbacknursery.ca

    This step is asking you to initiate contact with Outback Nursery by sending them an email. The email address provided, info@outbacknursery.ca, is where you should send your message. When you send the email, you should introduce yourself, provide any necessary details, and specify the purpose of your communication. This step is essentially the first point of contact with the nursery, allowing them to know who you are and why you are reaching out to them.
